Md Mokarram Chowdhury

Ph.D. Student in Computer Science | Iowa State University

I am a researcher in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, focusing on model compression, efficient deep learning, and large language models. My work aims to make AI systems more accessible by reducing computational requirements while preserving performance.

In our recent work, we developed importance-aware low-rank compression techniques for LLMs, achieving state-of-the-art compression ratios on mathematical reasoning and code generation tasks. We further investigated the broader implications of compression in Decomposed Trust, analyzing its impact on privacy, adversarial robustness, fairness, and ethical alignment.

Building on this, I am currently working on mechanistic interpretability of large language models, aiming to understand how safety, alignment works at the mechanistic level.
